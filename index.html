Projects

  • Project-1 description

    My friends and I are currently helping stray dogs by providing them with food and water and shelter if possible. We realised that they’re being treated horribly by the people who pass by and have rocks or water thrown at them by residents of the nearby slums. This gave us a feeling of the need to help them in any which way we can. We collected funds from our fellow classmates and friends with the intention of buying dog food. Since everybody was so willing to donate and help us go around and feed the dogs, we understood the value of man’s best friend as well as the importance of what we were doing. We visit Carter Road and feed the dogs roaming the promenade and laying down in the heat and pour some water in a bowl for them. At my age, we can’t make a big change to any of the situations in the world, but helping out a little made me feel good.
